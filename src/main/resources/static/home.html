Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Pawdcast

What is Pawdcast and how does it work?

Pawdcast is a comprehensive pet care platform that helps pet owners manage their pet's health, training, and overall well-being. You can create detailed pet profiles, access breed-specific information, get expert training tips, and track medical records - all in one place.

Is Pawdcast completely free to use?

Yes! Pawdcast offers a completely free basic plan that includes pet profile creation, breed information, basic training tips, and health tracking. We believe every pet deserves excellent care, which is why our core features will always remain free.

How many pets can I add to my account?

You can add unlimited pets to your Pawdcast account! Whether you have one furry friend or a whole family of pets, our platform can accommodate all of them with individual profiles and customized care plans.

Is my pet's data secure and private?

Absolutely! We take data privacy very seriously. All your pet's information is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your personal data with third parties without your explicit consent. Your privacy is our top priority.

Can I access Pawdcast on mobile devices?

Yes! Pawdcast is fully responsive and works perfectly on all devices - smartphones, tablets, and desktops. You can access your pet's information anytime, anywhere with our mobile-friendly web interface.

How accurate is the breed information provided?

Our breed information is curated by veterinary experts and experienced breeders. We provide comprehensive details about temperament, exercise needs, grooming requirements, health considerations, and training tips for over 200 dog breeds, with regular updates based on the latest research.

Can I share my pet's profile with my veterinarian?

Yes! You can easily generate and share medical reports with your veterinarian. This feature helps your vet get a complete picture of your pet's health history, making consultations more efficient and effective.

What if I need help using the platform?

We offer 24/7 customer support through multiple channels. You can reach us via email at Info.pawdcast@gmail.com or contact our support team directly. We're always here to help you make the most of Pawdcast!
